Background

Xue Hao  ·  薛皓

I'm a final-year Bachelor's student in Digital Product Design at Universiti Tunku Abdul Rahman (UTAR), Malaysia — Faculty of Information and Communication Technology.

My focus is at the intersection of intelligent hardware, embedded systems and AI applications. I'm genuinely excited about closing the gap between raw sensor data and useful, deployable systems — from designing hardware circuit topologies and writing Python drivers, to building the Vue.js dashboards that make the data make sense.

I believe good engineering means caring about every layer: the physical circuit, the firmware, the backend logic, the API design, and the UI that a real person actually uses.

Theory of Inventive Problem Solving (TRIZ)

I use it to analyse trade-offs in hardware design, identify root causes of sensor failures, and structure solutions for problems that seem to have no clean answer.
